About Us

Our Story

We started with the idea of just being the average kakanin/national delicacies seller to make money on the side in order to support ourselves through college. We realized that our country's national delicacies are a massive part of our nation's identity and so we decided to help preserve our heritage. With the passion, aspiration, and motivation, we are here to preserve and help the new generation to experience and enjoy what our nation has in terms of delicacies. And with this, we are hoping to pass down our nation's delicacies through multiple generation and never forget the identity our nation and its people has.

Our Mission

To provide high quality Filipino delicacies for the younger and more modernized generation to enjoy and appreciate our heritage.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading providers of an important branch of our culture, which are the delicacies and to preserve such delicacies for generations to come.

About Our Products

The products we serve are guaranteed to be made with fresh ingredients and made in facilities that are well maintained and regularly cleaned to ensure safe consumption.Our ingredients are sourced from local farmers and sellers in order to keep the ingredients authentic and to help the local farmers.
